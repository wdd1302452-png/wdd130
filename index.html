Lagos

Lagos is a state in southwestern Nigeria. It is bordered by Ogun State to the north and east, the Republic of Benin to the west, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. The state is a global center of commerce, entertainment, technology, and finance, serving as the economic heartbeat of West Africa and a major hub for international trade. Lagos City, the former national capital and most populous urban area, is home to the innovative Eko Atlantic City—a groundbreaking reclaimed land development that stands as a symbol of modern African urban planning and luxury.
